About Me

Hello, I'm not a wizard, but if you watch me for long enough, you might think that what I do is magic. My name is Jimmy Dwyer, and I'm originally from Virginia. I moved to Chicago to try and find out, "Hey, am I funny?" I quickly found out I was, and I did a ton of open mics to more than my own success. I went ahead afterwards and started doing classes at Improv Olympic and Second City.


After completing programs at both schools of comedy thought, my bills started to pile up. My hourly pay as a barista at Starbucks didn't pay the bills quite the way that was expected and I decided it was time to use my degree. I got a job as a book publicist in Chicago, getting clients in magazines, radio, and even TV shows! But, still, the desire to learn and grow continued (this time with enough money) and I sought new pursuits.

So, now I'm enrolled in the NU Coding Bootcamp, and I couldn't be happier. Please read this.
