About Me

Hello, I'm not a wizard, but if you watch me for long enough, you might think that what I do is magic. My name is Jimmy Dwyer, and I'm originally from Virginia. I moved to Chicago to find out what I was best at - turns out it's coding and cooking. In 2018 I enrolled in a full-stack coding bootcamp at Northwestern University.

After countless hours staring into the not-so-neon of Visual Studio Code, I finally started understanding. Concepts and languages like front-end design, Node.js, and MySQL started to make sense. I feel confident as a back-end developer, but can support teams in any role necessary.

So, now I'm almost finished at my NU Coding Bootcamp, and I couldn't be happier. Please read this.
